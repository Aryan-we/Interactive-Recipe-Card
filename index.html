Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup boiling water

Cooking Instructions

  1. 1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. 2. In a large bowl, stir together flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. 3. Add milk, oil, eggs, and vanilla extract; beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  4. 4. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin). Pour batter into prepared pans.
  5. 5. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. 6. Cool cakes in pans for 10 minutes, then remove from pans and cool completely on wire racks.
  7. 7. Frost with your favorite frosting and enjoy!

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Serving Size: 12 servings
